About Us

The Progressive Pathways Party (PPP) is a student-led movement at ICS College focused on empowering students through integrity, service, and innovation. We stand for leadership that uplifts, opportunities that transform, and unity that strengthens.

Our Services

  • Mentorship & Leadership Training
  • Talent Showcases & Competitions
  • Academic & Wellness Support
  • Student Advocacy & Representation

Financial Support & Bursaries

We believe that no student should be left behind due to financial constraints. Through the Progressive Pathways Party, comrades can:

  • Request bursary support for tuition and essential academic materials
  • Access emergency financial assistance for critical needs
  • Apply for our internal PPP Comrades Fund—run by students, for students
  • Receive guidance on external scholarship and funding opportunities

To request support, reach out through our contact form or visit our help desk during PPP events and forums.
